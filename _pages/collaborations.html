► Deep learning-based GPR inversion

Ground penetrating radar (GPR) techniques are widely used in planetary exploration, archaeological prospecting, geological hazard assessment, etc. We developed a GPR inversion operator to support real-time and reliable imaging of near-surface permittivity and resistivity structures simultaneously.

Xue et al., 2024

► Earthquake monitoring and prediction

Seismic electric signals (SES) are essential short-term precursors of earthquakes. We developed a Real-time Automatic Search Engine (RASE) for SES detection.

Xue et al., 2022, 2023
